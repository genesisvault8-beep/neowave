// HOW IT WORKS

Three steps.
Fully private.

No signups. No installs. Just open, share, and talk.

STEP 01
🌊
Open a Wave
Hit Launch App, enter your name, set an optional password. Your Wave ID is generated instantly — no account, no email. Each Wave connects exactly two people, 1-on-1.
STEP 02
🔗
Share the Link
Copy your invite link and send it to whoever you want. They open it in any browser and join directly — the link is the only key.
STEP 03
⚡
Talk Freely
Messages, files, and typing indicators flow directly device-to-device. When everyone leaves, the Wave closes. Nothing is saved anywhere.
// FEATURES

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🔒
P2P Encryption
WebRTC DTLS encryption on every connection. Messages go directly between devices — no relay server reads your data.
📁
File Transfer
Send files up to 50MB peer-to-peer. Images, APKs, documents — chunked and streamed directly to the recipient with live progress.
⌨️
Live Typing
See what the other person is typing in real time as they type it. The Wave feels alive — not like waiting for a letter.
🔑
Password Protection
Lock your Wave with a password. Only people who know it can join — even if they have the link.
👥
1-on-1 by Design
Every Wave connects exactly two people, directly. No groups, no extra guests — just a clean, private line between you and them.
📱
Works Everywhere
Mobile, tablet, desktop. Android, iOS, Windows, Mac. Any browser that supports WebRTC — which is all of them.
// PRIVACY SHIELD

Nothing stored.
Ever.

Most "private" messengers still run your messages through their servers. NeoWave doesn't. There's no database, no logs, no message history — because there's no server to store any of it.

When the Wave closes, it's gone. No backups. No recovery. That's the point.
